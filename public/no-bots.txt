No Bots Policy

Automated access to this website is not authorized except to read robots.txt and no-bots.txt.

Do not use bots, scripts, agents, crawlers, scrapers, or automated tooling to access this site without written permission from Hack Club.

Disallowed automated uses include:

- scraping
- indexing
- data mining
- bulk downloading
- mirroring
- training-data collection
- automated summarization or republication
- automated vulnerability scanning
- automated form submission

All other automated access is disallowed unless Hack Club gives written permission.
